Grafide Designs

The education initiative.

Grafide Designs is the learning platform arm of the Grafide brand. Built on the belief that design education is fragmented — too many YouTube tutorials, not enough structure, no accountability — we built a platform that changes that.

Structured courses. Embedded resources. Level locking. Assessments. Verified certificates. Everything a serious design student needs, in one place, free to start.

The platform covers five industry-standard tools across three progressive levels each. Complete all three levels, pass the assessments, and earn a certificate that anyone can verify publicly.
