1. What We Collect

When you use Grafide, we collect the following categories of information:

Data TypeWhat It IncludesWhy
Account DataName, email address, hashed passwordTo create and manage your account
Progress DataLessons completed, quiz scores, level statusTo track your learning journey
Certificate DataName, course completed, date issued, certificate IDTo issue and verify certificates
Usage DataPages visited, time on platform, browser typeTo improve the platform
CommunicationsMessages sent via contact form, newsletter emailTo respond to enquiries

We do not collect payment information directly. We do not sell your personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Create and maintain your Grafide account
  • Track and display your learning progress
  • Issue and verify completion certificates
  • Send account-related communications (e.g. password resets)
  • Send newsletters if you have subscribed (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve course content and platform features
  • Respond to contact form submissions

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in MongoDB — both locally during development and via MongoDB Atlas (cloud) in production. Data is stored within secure, access-controlled environments.

Passwords are never stored in plain text. We use BCrypt hashing to store password data securely.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Infrastructure and hosting providers who process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Certificate verification: Certificate ID, student name, course name, and issue date are publicly accessible via the verification endpoint for anyone with the certificate ID

5. Cookies

Grafide uses minimal cookies and browser storage. We use localStorage to store your authentication token and session data locally on your device. This data does not leave your browser except when making authenticated API requests to our backend.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. See our full Cookie Policy for details.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent for newsletter communications at any time
  • Data portability — request a copy of your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@grafide.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Grafide is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include BCrypt password hashing, JWT-based authentication, and HTTPS encryption in production.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights:

Email: privacy@grafide.com

Or use our contact form and select "Privacy" as the subject.
