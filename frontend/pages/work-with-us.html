Partnerships

Build something with us.

Grafide is building the go-to platform for graphics design education. We're open to partnerships that extend our reach, enrich our content, or bring new tools to our students.

Brand Partnerships

Integrate your design tool or software into Grafide's curriculum. Reach an audience actively learning to use the tools you build.

Educational Institutions

Bring Grafide into your school, college, or training programme. We offer structured content that complements formal design education.

Affiliate & Referral

Refer students to Grafide and earn. If you run a design community, blog, or YouTube channel, let's talk about an affiliate arrangement.

Content Licensing

Want to use Grafide's course content for your own platform or programme? We're open to licensing discussions.

Partner Application

Tell us about your organisation and what you have in mind.

Content & Tutors

Share your expertise.

Grafide is built on quality content. If you're a practising designer, educator, or specialist who wants to contribute lessons, tutorials, or a full course — we want to hear from you.

Become a Tutor

Create and publish your own courses on Grafide. Set your curriculum, write lessons, and curate resources under your name.

Contribute a Lesson

Not ready for a full course? Submit a single lesson or tutorial to be reviewed and published under our editorial team.

Video Content

Have a YouTube channel covering design? We curate quality external videos into our lessons. Submit your channel for consideration.

Guest Editorial

Write for Grafide's editorial section. Deep dives, design theory, tool comparisons, career advice — if it helps designers, we're interested.

What we look for

Practical, hands-on knowledge of the tool or topic
Clear, structured communication — you can explain things step by step
Original content — we don't republish content that already exists elsewhere
Commitment — tutors are expected to maintain and update their content
